How much water is okay?

Drinking an adequate amount of water daily is crucial for maintaining good health. While specific water intake needs can vary based on factors like age, weight, and activity level.

A general guideline is to aim for about 8 glasses of water per day, which equates to around 2 liters.

This amount helps to keep the body hydrated, supports various bodily functions such as digestion and temperature regulation, and contributes to overall well-being.
